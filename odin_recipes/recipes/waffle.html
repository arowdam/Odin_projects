Waffle

Description

A homemade waffle recipe using a waffle iron.

Ingredients

  • waffle mix
  • spray oil
  • water

Steps

  1. Pre-heat waffle iron.
  2. Add water to waffle mix until yogurt-like consistency.
  3. Add appropriate amount of mixture to waffle iron.
  4. Close waffle iron and wait for steam.
  5. Wait until steam stops and remove waffle.
  6. Enjoy!
