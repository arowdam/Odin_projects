Turkey burger

Description

A turkey burger recipe made using raw ground turkey.

Ingredients

  • ground turkey
  • yellow onion
  • garlic powder
  • adobo seasoning
  • salt

Steps

  1. Season ground turkey in bowl then mix.
  2. Take portions of the ground turkey and mold into ball.
  3. Set the balls aside and prepare pan heating on low.
  4. Cook/flatten balls on pan till brown and bloodless.
  5. Enjoy!
