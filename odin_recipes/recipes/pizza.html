Pizza

Description

A boboli style pizza recipe using a pre-made crust

Ingredients

  • base crust
  • pizza sauce
  • cheddar cheese
  • mozzerella cheese
  • cooked ground turkey
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • italian seasoning
  • red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees f.
  2. On cookie sheet, add layer of sauce to pizza.
  3. Season sauce with garlic and onion powder, spread sauce.
  4. Add the two cheeses to desired amount overtop.
  5. Add ground turkey atop the cheese and lightly add italian seasoning overtop the whole.
  6. Bake pizza for 10 minutes in oven.
  7. Enjoy!
